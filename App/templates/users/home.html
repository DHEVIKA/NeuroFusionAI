About NeuroFusionAI

Deep Learning Medical Analysis

Our custom 2D convolutional neural network separates scans into 6 classes with high accuracy and localizes lesions using Grad-CAM attention overlays.

Glioma & Meningioma

Gliomas arise from glial cells and meningiomas from the meninges. The system performs accurate detection, mapping tumors even at sub-centimeter bounds.

Neurocytoma & Normal

Accurately identifies rare Central Neurocytomas located in ventricles, as well as labeling completely healthy, normal scan control groups.

Pituitary & Schwannoma

Detects Pituitary adenomas compressing optic paths, and Schwannomas affecting cranial acoustic nerve sheaths, indicating localized risk scores.
